Privacy Policy for JomRezeki

Last updated: February 26, 2024

Introduction

This privacy policy describes how JomRezeki ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Camera Permission

Our app requires camera access to:

  • Allow users to take profile pictures
  • Capture documents for KYC verification
  • Upload resume documents

We only access your camera when you explicitly choose to take a photo or scan documents within our app. We do not access your camera in the background or without your knowledge.

How We Use Camera Data

Photos taken through the app are:

  • Used only for the specific feature you are using (profile picture, KYC, etc.)
  • Uploaded to our secure servers
  • Not shared with third parties except as necessary for the service (e.g., KYC verification)

Data Storage and Security

  • All photos and documents are stored securely
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • We regularly review our data collection and storage practices

Your Rights

You can:

  • Choose not to grant camera permissions (though this may limit some app features)
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Update or remove your profile pictures and documents
